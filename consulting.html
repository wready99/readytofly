Coaching & its advantages

Coaching is working with you, the client, to inspire you. With creative and thought-provoking processes, together we will maximize your personal and professional potential.

Because coaching is not just giving advice, ’fixing‘ or healing—although healing is often a great side-effect—the main advantage is transformation through your personal development. You're in the driver's seat when you work with a coach. You will discover what you want for yourself, determine what might be getting in the way, and how to overcome it.

What sets my coaching apart?

I'm trained in multi-dimensional speaking and listening; I listen for the meaning behind your words and your stories, whilst also listening for non-verbal cues and whatâ€™s not said. As your coach I am not constrained by your habits and belief systems that limit what is possible for you. I will support you to create a new awareness and perspectives to reflect your greatest self. I use a combination of facilitative and ontological techniques including goal setting, accountability and action, whilst addressing your ‘being,’ the person that you choose to be in any given situation. When you're aware that you can choose how you show up, you can vastly alter the outcome of any situation.

Create deep sustainable shifts

The ‘being-based’ approach creates deep, sustainable shifts that positively impact every area of your life. I'm trained in over 350 tools and guide you through discovery and reflection to support your journey of transformation. Between sessions, you'll continue your discovery and development through jointly-designed activities known as ‘practice areas’. Each coaching partnership is tailored to your needs as they evolve—it is your journey and ultimately you design your program.

Together, we will:

  • Identify the goals that you want to achieve from coaching and design detailed project plans with actions and milestones to achieve the goals.
  • Identify your best characteristics—the unique combination of qualities that you bring into a room that set you apart from others—and establish how you can live from your personal best 90% of the time.
  • Distinguish your predictable, default ways of being to understand how they serve you, and how they hold you back.
  • Establish your power over them rather than letting your default ways of being run the show.
  • Become aware of your ‘stories’ that impact the way you approach your life.
  • Identify your winning strategy and how it might be evolved in order to elicit the future that you desire.
